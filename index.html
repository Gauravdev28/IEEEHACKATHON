About IEEE YESIST12

IEEE YESIST12 (Youth Endeavour for Social Innovation using Sustainable Technology) is a global innovation initiative for students and young professionals. It empowers the next generation to build technology-driven solutions for social impact.

Everything we do is strictly aligned with the **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)**. Top-performing teams from this preliminary round will have the chance to progress toward the **International Grand Finale in Indonesia**.

Hackathon Tracks

Innovation Challenge

Develop technical solutions for sustainable land ecosystems. This track focuses on using AI, GIS, and Remote Sensing to protect and restore Life on Land (SDG 15). Ideal for those with strong technical and analytical skills.

Maker Fair

A track dedicated to hardware and software prototypes that solve local community problems. Whether it's a smart irrigation system or a community health app, this track is for the makers and builders focused on direct social impact.

Special Track

Explore the potential of emerging technologies like Blockchain, IoT, and AR/VR. This track challenges participants to implement high-end tech solutions for industry-specific problems and governance models.

WePOWER Track

Dedicated to inclusive and sustainable energy solutions. To promote gender diversity in STEM, this track **requires at least one female member** in every team. Focused on narrowing the gender gap in technology.
